Who is Angel Love

  • Angel is an Accredited Financial Counselor with an MBA and over 10 years experience in finance/business administration combined with years of valuable non-profit service. Proven dynamic communicator with a unique perspective of military, civilian and corporate clientele. Exceptional organizational, interpersonal skills, resource management, analytical and problem solving skills.

  • She's the Author of “How to Raise Your Credit Score in 10 Easy Steps” and has participated in several speaking engagaments focusing on a range of financial topics. In her private practice, Angel offers personal financial counseling on credit report/credit score management, home-buying, debt elimination, choosing insurance, preparing for retirement, estate planning, and understanding investments. She also helps small business strategize to reduce costs, increase efficiency, and stimulate bottom-line growth.

  • As a Financial Planning Counselor serving Fort Myer and the Pentagon through the Army Career and Alumni Program (ACAP), Angel is responsible for conducting DOD Financial Planning Workshops and Financial Management Seminars. Providing confidential financial planning sessions and support to transitioning military personnel and their families. She reviews her client’s financial circumstances and assists with creating financial plans including but not limited to short and long-term financial strategies.

  • Angel got her start on Wall Street years ago where she developed a passionate interest in all things financal. In 2009 she began contracting for MHN Government Services, first as a Fellow for the FINRA Investor Education Foundation Program, then as a Military Family Life Consultant - Personal Financial Counselor (MFLC-PFC). Assignments include Ft. Carson, Colorado; Schriever Air Force Base, Colorado; The Pentagon, Washington, DC; Ft. Drum, New York; Ft. Bragg, North Carolina. She has served as a Financial Consultant for Military OneSource in Arlington, Virginia. Since joining the Ft. Myer, VA ACAP team as Financial Planning Counselor, she's also worked with the Ft. Campbell, KY ACAP team.
